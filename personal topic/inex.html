Imagination station

The Imagination Station is a 10,000 sq. ft. of playground fun designed by area children and constructed by the community located at Mulligans Hollow behind the Tri-Cities Family YMCA in Grand Haven. Open all year long dawn to dusk. Free admission and a barrier-free space for children.
